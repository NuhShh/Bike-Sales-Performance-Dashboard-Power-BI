# 🚴‍♂️ Bike Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📊 Overview
This project presents an interactive **Sales Performance Dashboard** built using Power BI to analyze bike sales data across multiple countries, product categories, and customer segments. The goal is to transform raw transactional data into meaningful insights that support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance across regions and products  
- Identify key revenue drivers and top-performing categories  
- Understand customer segmentation (gender, age group)  
- Provide actionable insights for business strategy  

---

## 🗂️ Dataset
Source: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/sadiqshah/bike-sales-in-europe/data

The dataset contains transactional sales data with 18 columns, including:

- Date (Day, Month, Year)  
- Customer demographics (Age, Gender, Age Group)  
- Location (Country, State)  
- Product hierarchy (Category, Sub-category, Product)  
- Sales metrics (Revenue, Profit, Cost, Order Quantity, Unit Price)  

---

## ⚙️ Data Preparation
- Cleaned and validated dataset to ensure consistency  
- Created a custom **Date Table** for time-based analysis  
- Built a structured **data model (fact & dimension tables)**  
- Standardized data types for accurate calculations  

---

## 📐 Data Modeling & DAX
- Developed key measures:
  - Total Revenue  
  - Total Profit  
  - Profit Margin  
  - Total Quantity  
- Implemented time-based analysis using Date Table  
- Applied **Top N filtering** for product performance  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Page 1: Executive Sales Overview
- KPI summary (Revenue, Profit, Margin, Quantity)  
- Sales trend over time  
- Revenue distribution by country  
- Product category performance  
- Customer segmentation by gender  

---

### 🔹 Page 2: Product Analysis
- Top 10 products by revenue  
- Category vs profitability comparison  
- Detailed product-level breakdown  
- Interactive filtering for deeper analysis  

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- Sales performance is driven by a limited number of high-performing products and markets  
- Revenue is concentrated in specific regions, indicating potential dependency  
- Profitability varies across product categories  
- Customer contribution is relatively balanced across segments
